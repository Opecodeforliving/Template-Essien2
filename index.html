    About Us

    Established as Finance Company, Debnoch Capital focuses on Development Finance, Fund Management, and Project & Infrastructure Finance with particular interest among the higher end bar of the SME Entrepreneur business space in Sub-Saharan Africa, with specific regional interest being on West, East and Southern Africa. We're presently keen on these sectors across board, ranging from Manufacturing, Banking & Finance, FinTech, Agribusiness, Energy & Power, Oil & Gas, Real Estate, Education and Healthcare.
